Medicaid Expenditure Projection Report

Consolidated report and visualization package aligned to the project's Output & Visualization requirements.

Project Scope

This project forecasts Medicaid expenditures for Michigan and the United States using annual data from 2013 to 2025, then generates 10-year projections for 2026 to 2035 with 95 percent confidence intervals.

  • State forecast target: Michigan
  • National forecast target: United States
  • Selected final model for both geographies: Prophet
  • Evaluation window: 2023 to 2025
